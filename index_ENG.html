Slave trade from the port of A Coruña



The memory of A Coruña conveniently forgets the action of slavers from reputable families. They made their fortune from the port of A Coruña with the slave trade: they transported Africans captured on their land to America in harsh conditions.

Properties and income that are still enjoyed today in Coruña have this abominable origin.
